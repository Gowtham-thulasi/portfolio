Blood Connect

Blood Donor Finding

Blood Connect is a life-saving platform designed to bridge the gap between blood donors and recipients during emergencies. The project aims to create a seamless and efficient system for locating and contacting registered blood donors when urgent transfusions are required. Users can register as donors, request blood, and track the status of their requests in real time. With its simple interface, quick notifications, and reliable database, Blood Connect ensures timely assistance, fostering a stronger network of support and saving countless lives.

Terrascope

Web Application | 3D Data Visualization | NASA Space Apps Challenge

Terrascope is an innovative web platform designed to transform Earth observation into an immersive 3D experience. Developed for the NASA Space Apps Challenge, it visualizes 25 years of global environmental change using real satellite data. The platform features eight interactive modules, each highlighting critical climate phenomena such as wildfires, rising temperatures, melting ice, and urban expansion. Through advanced 3D rendering, data analysis, and visual storytelling, Terrascope turns complex datasets into engaging narratives. By merging science, design, and technology, it inspires awareness, exploration, and action toward a sustainable future.

DOJO SCHEDULER

Web Application | Training Management System | Responsive Design

Dojo Scheduler is a dynamic web application designed to modernize and automate martial arts training center operations. Built using modern web technologies such as HTML5, CSS3, jQuery, Python, and Bootstrap, it provides a responsive, user-friendly platform for managing training programs, schedules, and student performance. The system eliminates the limitations of traditional manual processes by offering an online solution where users can easily access information about upcoming classes, training packages, and performance updates. Through efficient scheduling and real-time data presentation, Dojo Scheduler streamlines administrative tasks, reduces time consumption, and enhances overall operational efficiency for martial arts academies.
